Higher National Diploma (HND) in Business Computing (Systems)



Computing specialisation which I am going to talk about is Higher National Diploma (HND) in Business Computing (Systems). It is a 2-year module (depending on what university you will attend) based on technological aspect that organisations and businesses are using in terms of software and hardware. Particular course will give me an opportunity to learn how to program and code using Java, SQL, PHP and many other programming languages, create databases, creating professional websites and system analyses. During first year of studying this course, there are specific modules that each undergraduate student will learn. Those are database, problem-solving and programming, digital footprint, web development, computer systems and software engineering. After first year of study the following modules will be happening which are web programming, service management, group project, databases and quality and user-centred systems. During your study each module would be assessed on reports, essays, presentations, exams and practicals. This will give opportunity to each candidate to learn the industry and create contacts that will be crucial when you successfully graduate to get a good job in the future based on your gained qualifications.
